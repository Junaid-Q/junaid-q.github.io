Bio

Dr. Junaid Qadir is a researcher specializing in cybersecurity in IoT, particularly LoRaWAN networks. His work includes developing secure key management mechanisms and predicting postural stability using IMU data. He completed his PhD on Cybersecurity in LoRaWAN Networks, and has experience in AI, IoT & Security. He has served as a Postdoctoral Researcher at CNIT Genova Unige and is an active reviewer for the IEEE Internet of Things Journal.
