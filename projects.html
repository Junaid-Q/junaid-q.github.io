Research and Development Projects

  • 5G INDUCE Project (EU Horizon 2020) – Focused on developing a secure 5G edge architecture for industrial IoT systems, integrating virtualized network functions and low-latency communication.
  • IMU-based Center of Pressure Prediction – Designed and trained deep learning models (LSTM, GRU, CNN-BiLSTM, and TCN-Attention) for predicting postural stability using wearable IMU data.
  • Lightweight Key Management for LoRaWAN – Proposed an AES-128 and ECDH-based cryptographic scheme validated with Scyther to enhance IoT communication security.
  • Smart Grid Security and Privacy – Contributed to a book chapter addressing confidentiality, integrity, and device lifecycle protection mechanisms for modern power systems.
  • Underwater Wireless Sensor Networks (UWSNs) – Developed routing protocols for delay minimization and energy-efficient data transmission in dense UWSNs.
